--------------------------------------------------------------
版本 B: Option B 8章壓縮版(門永遠開 主旨)
--------------------------------------------------------------

主旨: 阿嬤的門永遠開著。整 8 章貓不是被「留下」,是被「不阻止」。
3字弧: 被救 -> 流浪 -> 自願回來
強點: Ch5 + Ch8 "門總是開的" 重複本身就是愛的語言


Ch2 - 門開著
  1. The door is open.                     門開著
  2. I see a garden.                       我看見一個院子
  3. I can go outside.                     我可以走出去
  4. Type: outside                         outside = 外面
  5. I walk to the door.                   我走向那扇門
  6. (review) door / garden / outside / walk


Ch3 - 街上
  1. The street is long.                   巷子很長
  2. I see a big dog.                      我看見一隻大狗
  3. I run back home.                      我跑回家
  4. Type: afraid                          afraid = 害怕
  5. The door is still open.               門還是開著
  6. (review) street / dog / afraid / still


Ch4 - 安靜的院子
  1. She plants the seeds.                 奶奶種種子
  2. I sit near her.                       我坐在她旁邊
  3. She does not touch me.                她沒摸我
  4. Type: quiet                           quiet = 安靜
  5. I feel safe near her.                 我覺得安全
  6. (review) seeds / near / quiet / touch


Ch5 - 雨又來了 (thesis 高潮 1)
  1. The rain falls again.                 又下雨了
  2. I jump on the window.                 我跳上窗台
  3. The door is always open.              門總是開著
  4. Type: stay                            stay = 留下
  5. I come back. She is quiet.            我回來了, 她很安靜
  6. (review) window / always / stay / back


Ch6 - 夏天迷路
  1. The summer is hot.                    夏天很熱
  2. I follow a butterfly.                 我跟著一隻蝴蝶
  3. I am lost in the dark.                我在黑暗中迷路
  4. Type: smell                           smell = 聞
  5. I smell food and walk home.           我聞到食物的味道,慢慢走回家
  6. (review) summer / follow / lost / smell


Ch7 - 她老了
  1. The wind is cool.                     風變涼了
  2. She looks tired.                      她看起來累
  3. I want to stay with her.              我想留下來陪她
  4. Type: old                             old = 老
  5. She is old. The door is open.         她老了,門還是開
  6. (review) wind / tired / old / open


Ch8 - 回家 (thesis 高潮 2)
  1. The night is cold.                    夜晚很冷
  2. I see a small light.                  我看到一盞小燈
  3. She is asleep. The door is open.      她睡著了,門開著
  4. Type: home                            home = 家
  5. I come back. I am home.               我回來了, 我在家
  6. (review) night / light / asleep / home



--------------------------------------------------------------
版本 C: Ch1 hierarchical 試驗(主+支+主 3 Unit)
--------------------------------------------------------------

主旨: 一章拆 3 個 Unit,各自獨立小故事,中間夾一段支線氛圍。
共 13 題 (原 6 + 新增 7)


Unit 1.1 (主線): 雨夜醒來
  Q1. I wake up and the rain is falling hard.
      我在雨夜醒來,雨下得很大
  Q2. My fur is wet. I feel very cold.
      毛濕了,很冷
  Q3. I have not eaten since yesterday. I am so hungry.
      昨天起沒吃,好餓
  Q4. I curl up against the wall.                  ⭐新增
      我蜷在牆角


Unit 1.2 (支線 = 純氛圍): 空巷子的夜
  全新 4 題, 不推主線, 學感官詞

  Q1. A leaf falls slowly.                         ⭐新增
      一片葉子慢慢落下
  Q2. The street is empty.                         ⭐新增
      巷子是空的
  Q3. I hear a far car.                            ⭐新增
      聽到遠處的車聲
  Q4. The road smells wet and old.                 ⭐新增
      濕路面的氣味


Unit 1.3 (主線): 慈祥的臉
  Q1. I see a big shadow. (type: scared)
      看到一個大影子, 嚇到
  Q2. She holds a blue umbrella.                   ⭐新增
      她撐著藍色的傘
  Q3. She has a kind face.
      她有一張慈祥的臉
  Q4. She opens the umbrella wide.                 ⭐新增
      她把傘撐開來
  Q5. (review) hard / cold / hungry / kind
      回顧本章關鍵字


新增的 14 個詞:
  支線氛圍 (11): leaf / fall / slow / empty / far / car / road / smell / wet / old / quiet
  主線過渡 (3):  wall / blue / umbrella / opens / wide
